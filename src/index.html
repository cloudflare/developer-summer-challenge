How to Participate

What you should know before entering the Cloudflare Developer Summer Challenge:

What is the Cloudflare Developer Summer Challenge?

The Cloudflare Developer Summer Challenge is an event where developers are challenged to build an application using at least two of the products from the Cloudflare developer platform. We're giving away swag boxes to the top 300 submissions.

What is this year's challenge?

You're challenged to build any application with at least two of these four products in the Cloudflare developer platform: Cloudflare Workers, Workers KV, Durable Objects, and Cloudflare Pages.

We will judge submissions and award swag boxes to the 300 participants who submit the most innovative projects. Limit 1 per person. (See full terms and conditions.)

We also encourage you to share what you built on Cloudflares Discord server, and our Twitter by tagging @CloudflareDev and using the #DeveloperChallenge hashtag.

How will submissions be judged?

Submissions will be based on three criteria:

  • Your project must satisfy the basic challenge requirements: it must incorporate at least two products of the platform and you must provide the URLs to its source code repository and a working, live demo. You must submit before November 1, 2021.
  • We will judge by your project's innovation. Projects that are unique and serve useful to users will be more likely to win the swag boxes.
  • We will prefer projects that use more of the Cloudflare platform. The more products you integrate, the better!
Whats the deadline for submitting?

Submissions are open today and will close on November 1st, 2021.

How do I submit my project?

After signing up, you will receive a confirmation email that contains a submission link. Please note that this link is your personalized link and should not be shared.

The submission form will require links to a live demo and a public repository with your source code. We also encourage you to share on our Twitter, Discord, and Built With Workers accounts.

Do I have to pay to participate?

No. You can get started with Cloudflares developer products for free.

What are the products in Cloudflares developer platform?

Cloudflares developer platform includes:

  • Cloudflare Workers is a serverless platform that allows you to deploy code instantly across the globe.
  • Workers KV is a global, low-latency, key-value data store.
  • Durable Objects provide low-latency coordination and consistent storage for the Workers platform.
  • Cloudflare Pages is a JAMstack platform for frontend developers to collaborate and deploy websites.
How many Cloudflare products do I need to use to receive the swag box?

You must use at least two of Cloudflares developer products: Cloudflare Workers, Workers KV, Durable Objects, and Cloudflare Pages. Bonus points for using all four!

Where can I look to for project inspiration?

We offer a few suggestions in the Cloudflare Summer Challenge blog post. We also maintain a number of tutorials and examples. Additionally, you may browse through our Built With Workers collection to see what other community members have created!

How will I know if my submission has been approved to receive the prizes?

Our team will review submissions and select winners between November 1, 2021 and December 31, 2021.

We will send you an email to congratulate you on your award! We will also forward a link to the vendor site so you can select the proper sizing, etc.

Winners will be contacted by January 1, 2022 or sooner.

How long will it take to receive my swag box?

Delivery times will vary based on location. The box may take several weeks to arrive.
