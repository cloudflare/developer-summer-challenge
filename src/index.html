FAQ

Common questions about the Cloudflare Developer Anti-Conference

What is the Cloudflare Developer Anti-Conference?

The Cloudflare Developer Anti-Conference is a recurring event where developers are challenged to build an application using at least two of the products from the Cloudflare developer platform. We're giving away swag boxes for successful submissions.

What is this year's challenge?

You're challenged to build any application with at least two of these four products in the Cloudflare developer platform: Cloudflare Workers, Workers KV, Durable Objects, and Cloudflare Pages.

Swag boxes will be awarded on a first-come, first-served basis, subject to approval of the Cloudflare staff. Limit 1 per person. We also encourage you to share what you built on Cloudflare’s Discord server, and our Twitter by tagging @CloudflareDev and using the #DeveloperChallenge hashtag.

What’s the deadline for submitting?

Submissions are open while supplies last.

You may revisit this page to see real-time updates on how many swag boxes remain.

How do I submit my project?

After signing up, you will receive a confirmation email that contains a submission link. Please note that this link is your personalized link and should not be shared.

The submission form will require links to a live demo and a public repository with your source code. We also encourage you to share on our Twitter, Discord, and Built With Workers accounts.

What is required for me to be eligible to receive a swag box?

You must successfully complete the challenge by building any application that uses Cloudflare Workers, Workers KV, Durable Objects, and Cloudflare Pages. You must complete the submissions form with all necessary information, before prizes run out. Swag will be sent on a first come, first serve basis. Successful completion is subject to approval of the Cloudflare staff. We also encourage you to share what you built on Cloudflare’s Discord server, and our Twitter by tagging .

Do I have to pay to participate?

No. You can get started with Cloudflare’s developer products for free.

What are the products in Cloudflare’s developer platform?

Cloudflare’s developer platform includes:

  • Cloudflare Workers is a serverless platform that allows you to deploy code instantly across the globe.
  • Workers KV is a global, low-latency, key-value data store.
  • Durable Objects provide low-latency coordination and consistent storage for the Workers platform.
  • Cloudflare Pages is a JAMstack platform for frontend developers to collaborate and deploy websites.
How many Cloudflare products do I need to use to receive the swag box?

You must use at least two of Cloudflare’s developer products: Cloudflare Workers, Workers KV, Durable Objects, and Cloudflare Pages. Bonus points for using all four!

What are some examples of collaborative applications I could build?

A grocery list, an interactive game, a chat room, a remote whiteboard, etc.

How often will submissions be reviewed?

Submissions will be reviewed by the Cloudflare staff on a rolling basis.

How long will it take to receive my swag box?

Delivery times will vary based on location. The box may take several weeks to arrive.

How will I know if my submission has been approved to receive the prizes?

Our team will be in touch with you following your submission. We will send you an email with additional information about the swag box, and a link to the vendor site so you can select the proper sizing, etc.
